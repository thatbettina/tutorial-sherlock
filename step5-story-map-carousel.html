Madurai, Tamil Nadu, India

October 2012. After landing on the edge of Kerala, I make my way to Madurai, home of one of the most famous South Indian temple and a friendly face—the aunt of a friend I recently met. She made chapati and puris for me and told me the story of her family and their old house. I went to the temple early in the morning before the sun had risen.

ISHA, Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, India

Photos are not allowed at ISHA, the ashram of Sadhguru. I went to bathe with the solid mercury lingam and to make contact with what people claim is a living guru. I spent weeks here meditating, practicing yoga, and eating delicious sattvic vegan food in silence twice a day. I circled back to ISHA three times during my two months in South India.
